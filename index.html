YW Tech Lab Portfolio Group


The YW Tech Lab is a four month long web development program, focusing on providing the digital skills to traditionally underrepresented or vulnerable populations. Upon completion of this program, graduates of each cohort may join the Portfolio group for an additional 10 week web development cirriculum before designing and developing websites with for clients.

For more information about the YW Tech Lab, click here.
