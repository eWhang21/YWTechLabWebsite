YW Tech Lab Portfolio Group


The YW Tech Lab Portfolio Group is a program created from a partnership between the YWCA and Northwestern Univserity. Upon completion of YWCA's 4 month YW Tech Lab program, graduates of each cohort may join the Portfolio group for an additional 10 week web design and development focused curriculum before joining the design consultancy and development team.
